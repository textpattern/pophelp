h1. Articles: Timestamp

The time an article or file is published or revised. It can either be (re)set to the moment the item is saved, or to any other time.

The boxes correspond to the following information:

Year / Month / Day
Hour(24hr) : Minute : Second

Content with timestamps greater than the current time (i.e., in the future) will not appear on the site and the respective RSS/Atom feeds until that time passes.
