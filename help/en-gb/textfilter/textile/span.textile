h1. Textile: Spans

Use the @%@ percent symbol to indicate a span.

The @<span>@ HTML tag is applied, along with whatever @class@, @style@, @id@ or @lang@ attributes are specified between the opening percent and the first character of the phrase:

bc. %(caps)SPAN% → <span class="caps">SPAN</span>

bc. %{color:red}some red text% → <span style="color:red">some red text</span>

p(alert-block information). *Note:* Span tags must open and close within a their parent block-class element to produce valid markup.
